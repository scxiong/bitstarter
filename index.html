Audio tour is nice when we need an interpretation of a visitor attraction. While using our full-fledged smartphones for this purpose, the available applications do not meet our expectation. They are usually free of charge but of incomplete information, or they are developed by small vendors without experitse in this erea. People deserve better app to acccompany their tours.

Talkintours is the platform that answers to this need of travelers. We make it beautifully easy to use. Besides that, we come along with the best audio tours providers in the world. When we make the quality tour information paid and easy to purchase, the barrier between the provider and user will be overcomed.

Talkintours can be used as conventional voice tours that let you easily walk and listen to professionally made interpretations. Advanced presetnation methods such as video and virtual reality will also be possible. Entries are identified by means of location and other identification means. Most importantly, it serves as a platform to exchange carefully made location based information. Indivicual or organization can sell information to target customers easily and with low cost. All information in Talkintours will be rated and the most highly rated ones will be always easy to find.
